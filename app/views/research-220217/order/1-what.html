Order the other party to do something

What do you want to request from the other party?

You can request that the other party provides information. This does not need to relate to a case management order.

What is a case management order?
A case management order is an instruction from the Employment Tribunal for you to do something by a certain date. These orders can include tasks such as:

  • providing a Schedule of Loss
  • disclosure of documents
  • creating a bundle of documents
  • exchanging witness statements
for example, non-compliance or general requests

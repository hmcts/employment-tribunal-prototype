Amend the claim

How do you want to amend your claim?

You can ask for the tribunal’s permission to amend your claim. This could be adding, removing or changing important details such as:

  • the people or organisations you’re claiming against
  • the types of claim you are bringing
  • the facts on which your claim or claims are based
  • the remedy you are asking the tribunal to award

If you wish to add another respondent (person or organisation) you can do that at the next step.

Your original claim details and other guidance can be found in your case documents (opens in a new window).

How to answer this question

  • Explain the words you wish to remove or change. If it is easier, you can upload a separate document showing the changes you want to make. If you do that, then show the ‘tracked changes’ if you can.

Do you have a valid reason why you do not have an ‘Acas early conciliation certificate’?

Under certain circumstances you can make a claim without an Acas early conciliation certificate.

One of the following reasons (or ‘exemptions’) will cover your specific circumstances and explain why you do not need a certificate for a respondent:

  • You’re making a claim with another person who already has an Acas early conciliation number
  • Acas do not have the power to provide early conciliation on some or all of your claim
  • The organisations or people you’re making a claim against has already been in touch with Acas
  • You’re making an unfair dismissal claim which contains an application for ‘interim relief’ - this is rare and only applicable for certain reasons such as trade union activities, acting as a workers' representative or dismissal arising from whistleblowing
! Warning If you tell us the wrong exemption (or it does not apply) your claim may be rejected.
You’ll need to provide an exemption later as part of your claim. If you’re not sure which one applies contact Acas.
